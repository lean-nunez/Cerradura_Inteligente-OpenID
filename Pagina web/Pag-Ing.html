Proyect of Robotics and Programming from 5th grade students

Introduction

Our proyect seeks to optimize security in access control systems through RFID technology (card or tag) and PIN authentication. We replace physical keys with a modern and secure system that can also be integrated with IoT and home automation.

General Objective

Design and develop a smart lock that increases security and convenience of access to physical spaces, integrating RFID, PIN, and LCD screen.

Specific Objectives

  • Implement authentication via RFID card
  • Add secondary validation through PIN code
  • Show information and system status on LCD screen
  • Optimize security and energy consumption

Materials

  • Arduino ONE
  • RFID RC522 Module
  • 16x2 LCD Screen
  • Servomotor
  • Buzzer and status LEDs

  • RFID reading failure due to interferences
  • Cloning of RFID cards
  • Mistakes in cross-validation PIN + RFID
  • Excesive energy consumption
  • Vulnerabilities in data storage
